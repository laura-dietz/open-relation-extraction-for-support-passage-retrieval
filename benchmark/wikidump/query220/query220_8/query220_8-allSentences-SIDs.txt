35	NBA TV offers basketball news every day , as well as programming showcasing basketball players ' individual lifestyles , life as a basketball team during an NBA season , famous games of the past , and live games typically four days a week during the NBA season . 
36	Live games on NBA TV are subject to local blackout restrictions , since NBA TV does not have exclusive broadcast rights to any of its games . 
37	Games carried by NBA TV are always also carried by each team 's local rights holder . 
38	The channel also shows international games , typically on Saturday evenings , with special emphasis on the Euroleague and the Maccabi Tel Aviv team from Israel . 
39	In April 2005 , the channel televised the Chinese Basketball Association finals for the first time . 
40	NBA TV carries at least 90 regular-season games per season , all broadcast in high definition , as well as some first-round playoff games . 
41	It also broadcasts WNBA games nationally ( subject to local blackout ) . 
42	The channel 's flagship program is NBA Gametime Live , a show in which a host and studio analysts go over the latest news , highlights , and look in at games in progress . 
43	The show is live six days a week , not airing on most Thursdays due to NBA 's coverage of The NBA on TNT . 
44	An edited one hour/thirty minute version of the broadcast is repeated throughout the late night/early morning hours . 
45	NBA TV HD is a 1080i high definition simulcast of NBA TV . 
46	All studio programs and original shows are shot in HD , and all live games and recent game rebroadcasts are shown in HD. 
47	For programs not available in HD ( such as older game footage ) , unique stylized pillarboxes are used , the NBA logo with " NBA TV " under it , or alternatively , just " NBA TV " sideways . 
48	Both are in black and gray . 
49	The studio host and analysts vary every night on NBA Gametime Live .
